About us

The Polish Club of Berkeley is an organization uniting all Polish people associated with University of California, Berkeley as well as promoting Polish culture among all community members on campus. We aim to celebrate Polish heritage of both Polish students studying abroad in the United States as well as those with Polish-American backgrounds. We welcome all undergraduates, graduates, faculty and staff to come to our events. Knowledge of Polish is not necessary :-)

If you're visiting Berkeley as a scholar or an exchange student, check in with us! We'd love to connect you with other Polish people from the area and keep the local community vibrant.
